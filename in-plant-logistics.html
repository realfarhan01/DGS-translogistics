In-Plant Logistics

IN PLANT LOGISITICS

At DGS Translogistics India Pvt Ltd, we provide efficient In-Plant Logistics solutions to streamline material movement, optimize workflow, and enhance productivity within manufacturing and production facilities. Our end-to-end solutions ensure seamless coordination between different stages of production, reducing downtime and improving efficiency.

Our In-Plant Logistics Services

  • Material Handling & Internal Transport: Safe and efficient movement of materials using conveyors, forklifts, and AGVs.
  • Just-in-Time (JIT) & Just-in-Sequence (JIS) Delivery: Timely supply of raw materials to minimize inventory costs.
  • Line-Feeding & Kitting: Pre-assembled kits and continuous material supply to production lines for faster assembly.
  • Warehouse & Inventory Management: Real-time tracking and automated storage systems for optimal stock levels.
  • Reverse Logistics & Waste Management: Efficient handling of returned materials and waste disposal.

Technology-Driven Efficiency

We leverage AI-driven analytics, RFID tracking, and IoT monitoring for real-time visibility into material movement. Our cloud-based WMS and TMS integrate seamlessly with ERP systems, ensuring data-driven decision-making and operational transparency.

Industries We Serve

We cater to industries requiring synchronized material flow, including automotive, FMCG, pharmaceuticals, electronics, and manufacturing.

Why Choose DGS Translogistics?

  • Customized Solutions: Tailored in-plant logistics for specific industry needs.
  • Cost & Time Efficiency: Reduced waste and optimized workflow.
  • Advanced Technology: AI-powered tracking and automation.
  • Safety & Compliance: Strict adherence to workplace safety and quality standards.
